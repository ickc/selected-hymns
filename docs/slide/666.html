1

Jesus, my Savior, to Bethlehem came,
耶穌，我救主，降生馬槽中，
Laid in a manger to sorrow and shame;
成憂患之子，來經歷苦痛；
O it was wonderful, blest be His name,
哦，這真希奇，值得我稱頌！
Seeking for me, for me:
祂來尋我，尋我！

Dying for me, for me!
祂死為我，為我！

Jesus, my Savior, the same as of old,
耶穌，我救主，永活不改變，

雖遭我拒絕，仍召不間斷，
Calling for me, for me!
祂來召我，召我！
