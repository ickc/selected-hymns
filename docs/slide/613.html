1

On toward the goal! Press on! Alone, yet unafraid;
當向標竿力前！雖然孤單，不變；
He cut the path, who beckons thee, On then, and undismayed.
那開路者現在召你，所以當前勿延。

2

On toward the goal! Press on! The eyes that are a flame
當向標竿力前！ 主眼目如火焰，
Are watching thee, what then are men? What matter praise, or blame?
正在看你；人算甚麼？ 何必管他喜厭！

3

On toward the goal! Press on! Look not behind thee now,
當向標竿力前！不要再望後面，
When just ahead lies His Well done, And crowns await thy brow.
因為前頭就是獎賞，主要賜給冠冕。

4

On toward the goal! Press on! Blind, deaf and sometimes dumb
當向標竿力前！塞耳、啞口、閉眼，
Along the blood-marked, uphill way, Hard after Christ, press on!
在崎嶇的血跡路上，緊隨基督向前！
