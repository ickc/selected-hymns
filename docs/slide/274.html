Come let us anew 讓我們再從頭；

1

Come let us anew
讓我們再從頭；
Our journey pursue,
走主路，齊向前；
Roll round with the year,
如年日，新更換；
And never stand still till the Master appear.
莫站立，莫耽延；直等到主顯現；
His adorable will let us gladly fulfill,
讓我們心樂意，使祂旨，得成全；
And our talents improve,
並改進人才幹，
By the patience of hope, and the labor of love,
藉盼望的忍耐和愛心的勤勉，
(Repeat the last line of each stanza)
（每節重唱最後一行）

2

Our life is a dream,
時間如影難捉，
Our time as a stream
生命如水急流，
Glides swiftly away,
轉眼就成已過；
And the fugitive moment refuses to stay,
光陰飛，縱即逝，不為我稍等候，
The Arrow is flown,
箭脫弦，不可追，
The moment is gone,
年日去，不可留，
The millennial year
千禧年神的國，
Rushes on to our view, and Etemity’s here!
快趕到我面前，那永世就起首。

3

O that each in the day
願人人都能在
Of His coming might say
主來時，向祂說：
“I have fought my way thro’,
當跑路已跑盡，
I have finish’d the work Thou didst give me to do!”
所信道已守住，美好仗已打過！
O that each from his Lord
願人人都能從
May receive the glad word,
主那裡，聽見說：
“Well and faithfully done,
又良善、又忠心，
Enter into My joy, and sit down on My throne!
可進入我喜樂，可同登我寶座。
