I would love to tell you what I think of Jesus 讓我向你述說主耶穌的寶貴，

About 關於

meter: 12.11.12.11. with chorus 12.11.12.11. 和

1

I would love to tell you what I think of Jesus
讓我向你述說主耶穌的寶貴，
Since I found in Him a friend so strong and true;
祂是我最忠信朋友、不改變；
I would tell you how He chang’d my life completely—
讓我向你述說耶穌將我帶回，
He did something that no other friend could do.
無人像祂救我脫離眾罪鍊。

Chorus 和

No one ever cared for me like Jesus,
無人像耶穌這樣照顧我，
There’s no other friend so kind as He;
無朋友像主這樣慈愛；
No one else could take the sin and darkness from me—
無人像祂能使我罪惡全解脫，
O how much He cared for me!
主愛我何等大哉！

2

All my life was full of sin when Jesus found me,
當我陷在罪中，耶穌來尋找我，
All my heart was full of misery and woe;
那時我心中充滿失望、痛苦；
Jesus placed His strong and loving arms around me,
主用祂慈愛大能膀臂懷抱我，
And He led me in the way I ought to go.
引導我走上那永生的道路。

3

Ev’ry day He comes to me with new assurance,
每一天祂重新證實祂的同在，
More and more I understand His words of love;
使我日漸領會祂愛的無限；
But I’ll never know just why He came to save me,
但我永不知祂為何救我脫害，
Till some day I see His blessed face above.
直到那日，當我親見祂榮臉。
