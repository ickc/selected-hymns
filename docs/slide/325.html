There’s a peace in my heart that the world never gave, 有平安在我心，非世界所能賜，

About 關於

meter: 12.8.2.9. with chorus 12.8.2.9. 和

1

There’s a peace in my heart that the world never gave,
有平安在我心，非世界所能賜，
A peace it can not take away;
這平安無人能奪去；
Tho’ the trials of life may surround like a cloud,
雖一生多試煉，如愁雲環繞我，
I’ve a peace that has come there to stay!
這平安仍在我心永居！

Chorus 和

Constantly abiding, Jesus is mine;
永遠住在我心，耶穌屬我；
Constantly abiding, rapture divine;
永遠住在我心，無窮喜樂；
He never leaves me lonely, whispers,
祂永不會離棄我，柔聲對我說：
O so kind: I will never leave thee, Jesus is mine.
我永不離棄你，耶穌屬我。

2

All the world seemed to sing of a Savior and King,
當平安滿我心，似乎舉世歌唱、
When peace sweetly came to my heart;
讚美我的君王、救主；
Troubles all fled away and my night turned to day,
眾愁苦皆逃避，黑夜變為白晝，
Blessed Jesus, how glorious Thou art!
何榮耀，何有福，我耶穌！

3

This treasure I have in a temple of clay,
當我在這地上，流浪歷盡滄桑，
While here on His footstool I roam;
這寶貝仍藏我瓦器；
But He’s coming to take me some glorious day,
哦，榮耀之大日，祂必來提接我，
Over there to my heavenly home!
回天上父家永享安息。
