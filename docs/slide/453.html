Beautiful, beautiful,
真美麗，真美麗，
Jesus is beautiful;
耶穌真是美麗，
And Jesus makes beautiful things of my life.
耶穌使我生命中事物美麗。
Carefully touching me,
關懷我，感動我，
Causing my eyes to see;
使我眼被開啟，
Jesus makes beautiful things of my life.
耶穌使我生命中事物美麗。
