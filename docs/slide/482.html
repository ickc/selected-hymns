Fade, fade each earthly joy; 今世福樂沒滅，

1

Fade, fade each earthly joy;
今世福樂沒滅，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Break every tender tie;
地上愛情斷絕，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Dark is the wilderness,
在這幽暗野地，
Earth has no resting place,
無處可以安息，
Jesus alone can bless;
惟有基督可倚，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！

2

Tempt not my soul away;
莫誘我心離開，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Here would I ever stay;
我願與主同在，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Perishing things of clay,
你這今世歡娛，
Born but for one brief day,
一時騙人之局，
Pass from my heart away;
從我心中離去，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！

3

Farewell, ye dreams of night;
去罷，今世幻夢，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Lost in this dawning bright;
我今已經清醒，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
All that my soul has tried
凡我從前所慕，
Left but a dismal void;
不過使我痛苦；
Jesus has satisfied;
惟主使我滿足，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！

4

Farewell, mortality;
去罷，短暫今時，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Welcome, eternity;
來罷，長久永世，
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
Welcome, O Loved and Blest,
來罷，可愛福氣！
Wecome, sweet scenes of rest,
來罷，甜美安息！
Welcome, my Savior’s breast;
來罷，恩主自己！
Jesus is mine.
基督屬我！
