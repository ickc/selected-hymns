Jesus, Jesus, Jesus! 耶穌，耶穌，耶穌！

1

Jesus, Jesus, Jesus!
耶穌，耶穌，耶穌！
Sweetest Name on earth;
地上最甜名；
How can I, a sinner,
像我這樣罪徒，
Come to know its worth?
竟識其恩情。

2

Oh! the sinful sorrow,
哦，我真覺羞愧，
Oh! the strangest shame,
哦，我真懊悔，
That I saw no beauty
從前我竟蒙昧，
In that sacred Name.
不知這名美。

3

Never felt the sweetness!
從未覺其甘甜，
Never knew the grace,
從未識恩典，
Never saw the love-pain
從未見祂容顏——
In that wounded face!
顯深切愛憐。

4

Never found the mystery
也從未曾領悟，
In that simple word—
這名的豐富；
Jesus, Jesus, Jesus,
耶穌，耶穌，耶穌！
Savior, Lover, Lord.
親愛的救主。

5

Now ’tis past and over.
我的虧欠、羞恥，
Gone my guilt and shame;
因主的引領，
Jesus, Jesus did it,
現今都已過去，
Glory to His Name!
榮耀歸祂名！

6

Wonderful compassion,
哦，奇妙的大愛，
Reaching even me;
竟然臨及我；
Bows my humbled spirit
我靈謙卑下拜，
In captivity.
作愛的俘虜。

7

Jesus! Jesus! Jesus!
耶穌，耶穌，耶穌！
Loved me in my shame.
愛不堪如我。
Oh! the joy and rapture
我今歡欣、鼓舞，
Of that sacred Name.
這名真寬博！
