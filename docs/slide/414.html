O Lord, Thy boundless love to me 主阿，你的無限大愛，

1

O Lord, Thy boundless love to me
主阿，你的無限大愛，
No thought can reach, no tongue declare;
我口難述，我心難領；
Oh, knit my thankful heart to Thee,
被你吸引，我心感戴，
And reign without a rival there;
讓你作王，無人可競；
Thine wholly, Thine alone I am;
願主愛火將我點起，
Lord, with Thy love my heart inflame.
完全獻上，單單歸你。

2

Oh, grant that nothing in my soul
願我裡面，別無他物，
May dwell but Thy pure love alone;
只讓你的純愛居住；
Oh, may Thy love possess me whole,
你愛佔有我的全部，
My joy, my treasure, and my crown;
作我喜樂、冠冕、財富；
All coldness from my heart remove;
所有冷淡消除淨盡，
May every act, word, thought, be love.
讓愛充滿每一言行。

3

O Love, how cheering is Thy ray,
愛的光輝使我歡欣，
All pain before Thy presence flies;
所有痛苦消蹤無影；
Care, anguish, sorrow, melt away,
掛慮、憂愁融化消盡，
Where’er Thy healing beams arise;
當你醫治光線射進；
Lord Jesus, nothing may I see,
主，我只要見你榮面，
And naught desire, or seek, but Thee.
別無他求，別無他願。
