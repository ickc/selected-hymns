Merciful and loving,
憐憫、慈愛、寬恕、
Suff’ring pain and loss,
溫柔又謙和；
All for us, the sinners,
為了罪人忍辱，
Going to the cross;
甘負十架軛；
Tasting death for all men,
捨命、流血、受苦，
Dying for our sins;
為救失喪者：
This is Christ the Savior,
這是基督耶穌，
What will you do with Him?
對祂你如何？
