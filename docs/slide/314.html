My Father is omnipotent, 我父獨一、超絕、全能，

About 關於

meter: 8.6.8.6. with chorus 8.6.8.6. 和

1

My Father is omnipotent,
我父獨一、超絕、全能，
And that you can’t deny;
人都無法否認；
A God of might and miracles—
祂是大能、奇事的神，
‘Tis written in the sky.
顯在天上星辰。

Chorus 和

It took a miracle to put the stars in place,
星宿陳列天空，乃是神蹟奇事，
It took a miracle to hang the world in space;
地球懸在太空，也是神蹟奇事；
But when he saved my soul,
但祂救我靈魂，
Cleansed and made me whole,
全人得醫治，
It took a miracle of love and grace!
更是祂恩、愛的神蹟奇事！

2

Though here His glory has been shown,
雖然祂榮耀已顯現，
We still can’t fully see
我們仍難盡見
The wonders of His might, His throne—
祂大能的作為無限，
‘Twill take eternity.
須永世纔完全。

3

The Bible tells us of His power
聖經處處告訴我們，
And wisdom all way through,
祂的智慧權能；
And every little bird and flower
一花一鳥，在在證明，
Are testimonies too.
祂的全智、全能。
