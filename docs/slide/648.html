Some day the silver cord will break, 有日銀煉將要折斷，

1

Some day the silver cord will break,
有日銀煉將要折斷，
And I no more as now shall sing;
我就不再如此歌唱；
But, O the joy when I shall wake
但我醒來何等喜歡，
Within the presence of the King!
竟然得以面見我王。

And I shall see Him face to face,
我要見祂面對著面，
And tell the story, saved by grace:
我要述說救我恩典；
And I shall see Him face to face,
我要見祂面對著面，
And tell the story, saved by grace.
我要述說救我恩典。

2

Some day my earthly house will fall,
有日地上帳棚傾覆，
I cannot tell how soon ‘twill be,
我也不知何日何載；
But this I know—my All in all
但我確知有個去處，
Has now a place with Him for me.
使我能以與主同在。

3

Or some day when my Lord will come,
或者有日救主再臨，
And called to meet Him I’ll be blest,
那時工作即將完畢；
He then will say to me, Well done,
得主稱許忠心僕人，
And I shall enter into rest.
我要進入祂的安息。

4

Some day, till then I’ll watch and wait,
有日；所以我要準備，
My lamp all trimmed and burning bright,
把燈剔亮，儆醒等待；
That when my Savior I will greet,
那日我得與主相會，
My faith will then be changed to sight.
永遠享受祂的同在。
