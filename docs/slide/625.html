In the Overcomer’s Name Be an overcomer too! 在祂得勝的名裡，是得勝者的秘密！

1

In the Overcomer’s Name Be an overcomer too!
在祂得勝的名裡，是得勝者的秘密！
Stand and put the foe to shame All the livelong battle through!
一生經歷諸戰役，站穩並羞辱仇敵。

2

Jesus is the Name to sound, Name to rally fainting hearts!
呼求耶穌得勝名，此名使你得復興；
To recapture forfeit ground, Lost awhile through Satan’s arts.
認識仇敵的詭計，重新收復你失地。

3

Backward drive the Evil One! Force him out of every hold;
回頭速將惡者驅，將他逐離每寸地；
Smite until the sinking sun Sets upon the warrior bold!
戰士，進攻不退縮，奮勇進攻到日落。

4

In the Overcomer’s Name Be an overcomer too!
在祂得勝的名裡，是得勝者的秘密！
Stand and put the foe to shame All the livelong battle through!
一生經歷諸戰役，站穩並羞辱仇敵。
