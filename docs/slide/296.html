My hope is in the Lord 我的希望是主，

About 關於

meter: 6.6.6.6. with chorus 6.6.6.6. 和

1

My hope is in the Lord
我的希望是主，
Who gave Himself for me,
祂將自己給我，
And paid the price of all
加略山上已付
My sin at Calvary.
贖價、免我罪過。

Chorus 和

For me He died,
祂為我死，
For me He lives,
祂為我活，
And everlasting life and light
永生和光已賜，
He freely gives.
使我因祂得活。

2

No merit of my own
我自己的功績，
His anger to suppress,
不能消神怒氣，
My only hope is found
我的唯一希冀，
In Jesus’ righteousness.
是在主裏的義。

3

And now for me He stands
祂今為我在天，
Before the Father’s throne,
站在父寶座前，
He shows His wounded hands,
將祂傷手展現，
And names me as His own.
為我說出美言。

4

His grace has planned it all;
恩典成就一切，
’Tis mine but to believe,
只要我肯相信，
And recognize His work
承認祂的超絕，
Of love, and Christ receive.
接受祂的救恩。
