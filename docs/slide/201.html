Break Thou the Bread of Life, 願主為我擘開

1

Break Thou the Bread of Life,
願主為我擘開
Dear Lord, to me,
生命的餅，
As Thou didst break the loaves
就像當日旁海
Beside the sea;
對眾所行。
Beyond the sacred page
靠這聖書引導，
I seek Thee, Lord;
我尋求你；
My spirit pants for Thee,
我主，神的活道，
O Living Word.
我渴慕你！

2

Thou art the Bread of Life,
願主為我擘開
O Lord, to me,
生命的餅，
Thy holy Word the truth
使我充滿主愛，
That saveth me;
記念主名！
Give me to eat and live
釋放我的思想
With Thee above;
脫離自己，
Teach me to love Thy truth,
好叫我的傾向
For Thou art Love.
都在乎你。

3

Oh, send Thy Spirit, Lord,
惟主解開真理，
Now unto me,
我纔明白，
That He may touch my eyes,
你的一切所是，
And make me see;
向我啟開；
Show me the truth concealed
使我與你同情，
Within Thy Word,
與你同行，
And in Thy Book revealed
使我逐日變化，
I see the Lord.
像你榮形。

4

Bless Thou the truth, dear Lord,
愛主，求你祝福
To me, to me,
你的真理，
As Thou didst bless the bread
像你為餅祝福
By Galilee;
在加利利；
Then shall all bondage cease,
叫我捆綁都脫，
All fetters fall,
鎖鍊都落，
And I shall find my peace,
自由為你生活，
My All in all.
為你工作。
