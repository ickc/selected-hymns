1

There’s a Man in the glory
榮耀中有一位，
Whose Life is for me.
祂生命是為我。
He’s pure and He’s holy,
祂聖潔又公義、
Triumphant and free.
得勝且超脫，
He’s wise and He’s loving,
祂智慧又慈仁，
How tender is He!
溫柔且同情。
His Life in the glory,
榮耀中的生命，
My life must be.
成了我生命；

2

There’s a Man in the glory
榮耀中有一位，
Whose Life is for me.
祂生命是為我。
He overcame Satan;
祂勝過了撒但；
From bondage He’s free.
祂完全解脫。
In Life He is reigning;
在生命中作王；
How kingly is He!
祂滿有權能！
His Life in the glory,
榮耀中的生命，
My life must be.
成了我生命。

3

There’s a Man in the glory
榮耀中有一位，
Whose Life is for me.
祂生命是為我。
In Him is no sickness;
在祂毫無疾病；
No weakness has He.
祂毫不軟弱。
He’s strong and in vigor,
祂剛強又有力，
How bouyant is He!
超越且堅定！
His Life in the glory
榮耀中的生命，
My life may be.
成了我生命。

4

There’s a Man in the glory
榮耀中有一位，
Whose Life is for me.
祂生命是為我。
His peace is abiding;
祂裡面有平安；
How patient is He!
耐心何廣博！
He’s joyful and radiant,
祂喜樂又發光。
Expecting to see
祂盼望看見：
His Life in the glory
榮耀中的生命，
Lived out in me.
從我得彰顯。
